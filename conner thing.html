Fazwell's Pizzeria — The Five

An alternate retelling of the tragic events that once haunted the cheerful lights and animatronic smiles. Names and details have been changed; the yellow rabbit still remembers.

Timeline — The Five Incidents

  • April, Year Unknown — Incident One
    A small guest vanished after the evening show. Surveillance footage later showed a shadow moving behind the yellow rabbit during cleanup.
  • June — Incident Two
    A staff member reported odd noises coming from the prop storage; a second disappearance followed. The yellow rabbit's stage routine had been altered that night.
  • August — Incident Three
    Two teenagers were found missing after a late-night dare. A discarded backstage badge hinted at tampering with the animatronic wiring.
  • October — Incident Four
    An employee who claimed they could 'hear voices' left abruptly; they were never seen again. On the last photo they took, the yellow rabbit appears out of frame.
  • December — Incident Five
    A fatal accident was reported during an overnight maintenance check. While labeled an accident in official logs, eyewitnesses whispered about something—someone—hidden inside the yellow rabbit suit.
Note: This page is a fictional, slightly altered retelling for creative purposes. It includes unsettling themes but avoids graphic detail.
