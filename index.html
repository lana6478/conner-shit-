Fazwell's Pizzeria — The Five

An alternate retelling of the tragic events that once haunted the cheerful lights and animatronic smiles. Names and details have been changed; the yellow rabbit still remembers.

Timeline — The Five Incidents

  • April, Year Unknown — Incident One
    Guests recalled the lights flickering as the last show ended. A child followed what they described as a "warm humming" into the service hall. When staff reviewed the cameras, frames were missing—corrupted—and the yellow rabbit was seen facing the wrong direction between cuts.
  • June — Incident Two
    Maintenance reported finding deep scratches on the inside of the rabbit's suit casing. That same week, a young guest disappeared near the photo booth. Witnesses described hearing tapping from inside the stage lift long after power was shut off.
  • August — Incident Three
    Two teens attempting to film an after-hours exploration vanished. Their camera was recovered, its final footage showing the rabbit jerking toward the lens in a series of unnatural, stuttering motions—almost as if someone inside was trying to get out.
  • October — Incident Four
    A night guard left a panicked voice message claiming the rabbit "stood by the office door even though it hadn't moved in years." He resigned and fled town the next morning; his belongings and badge remained untouched in his locker.
  • December — Incident Five
    During an overnight inspection, emergency responders discovered the rabbit suit propped upright in the main dining room. The suit was warm to the touch despite the building losing heat hours earlier. An inspector later reported hearing slow breathing from inside the suit when alone.
Note: This page is a fictional, slightly altered retelling for creative purposes. It includes unsettling themes but avoids graphic detail.

Key Figure — "Sunny" the Yellow Rabbit

SR

Originally built as a cheerful party host, Sunny became infamous after staff discovered its internal memory core had logged unauthorized "manual overrides" long after its operator key was retired. Some claim the rabbit reacts to names spoken near it.

Role: Stage mascot, photo-op host
